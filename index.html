About CRK Party

The Compatriots For Reforms Kenya (CRK) Party is committed to transforming Kenya through progressive policies, transparent governance, and citizen-centered development.

We believe in the urgency of now - the time to act decisively for a better Kenya is today.

  • ✓ Transparency in Government
  • ✓ Economic Development
  • ✓ Social Justice
  • ✓ Sustainable Development
  • ✓ Citizens' Empowerment

Our Manifesto

📚 Education

Quality, accessible education for all Kenyans. Investment in STEM, vocational training, and digital literacy.

💼 Economy

Job creation, SME support, agricultural modernization, and fair taxation to build a prosperous economy.

🏥 Healthcare

Universal healthcare access, affordable medicines, and improved medical facilities across all counties.

🌍 Environment

Sustainable environmental practices, green energy transition, and conservation of natural resources.

⚖️ Justice

Transparent governance, anti-corruption measures, and rule of law for all citizens.

🏘️ Infrastructure

Modern roads, reliable electricity, clean water, and digital connectivity for all regions.
